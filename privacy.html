Privacy Policy

Personal Data

Who A'int Following Me Back does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data, browsing history, or account information. The extension only reads the list of follower and following usernames displayed in the social media dialog box when the user activates it.

All processed data (follower and following usernames) is stored locally on the user’s device and is never sent to any external servers. The extension does not identify users, track behavior, or associate data with personal accounts.

Data Usage

The collected data is used solely for comparison and display within the extension, so users can check their follower and following lists. Data remains stored locally unless the user manually clears it.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. It relies only on Chrome’s (Chromium) local storage APIs for saving user data.

Consent

By using this extension, you acknowledge and agree that all data stays on your local device and is not collected by the developer or shared with third parties.
