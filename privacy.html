Privacy Policy

Overview

Who A'int Following Me Back (“the Extension”) is designed with privacy as a top priority. The Extension operates entirely on your device and does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data or account information.

User Data

The Extension does not access or interact with any social media websites, including Instagram. Instead, users manually upload their own Instagram data (in the ZIP file provided by Instagram’s “Download Your Information” feature).

This ZIP file remains entirely within your browser session — the Extension does not:

  • Send the data to any external servers
  • Store the data locally or remotely
  • Share the data with the developer or any third party
Data Processing

The uploaded ZIP file is processed temporarily within your browser’s memory to compare your “followers” and “following” lists. The results are displayed immediately on the page for your reference. Once you close or refresh the page, all data is cleared automatically.

Third-Party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. It runs fully client-side, relying only on standard browser capabilities.

Consent

By using the Extension, you acknowledge and agree that:

  • You voluntarily upload your own Instagram data
  • All processing occurs locally within your browser
  • No information is collected, stored, or transmitted by the developer
Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer. Email in the footer.
